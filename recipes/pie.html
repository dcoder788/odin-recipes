Meat Pie

Description

A tasty meat pie recipe that can be easily baked with beef, pork and potato.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Water
  • Pie crust

Steps

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  2. Cook the meat with the water, onion, garlic, and seasonings.
  3. Mix in the potatoes, then fill the pie crust. Cover with the top crust.
  4. Bake until the crust is browned.
